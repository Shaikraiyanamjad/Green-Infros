About Us

Powering Homes & Businesses With Smart Solar and Roofing Solutions

Green Enfros is a trusted name in solar and roofing innovation. Established in 2008, we began by delivering high-quality solar systems and have expanded into durable, weather-resistant roofing services. Our mission is to provide sustainable, cost-effective, and long-lasting solutions that power your home while protecting it from above.
